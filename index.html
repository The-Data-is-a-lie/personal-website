PF1e Character Generator

An open-source Pathfinder 1e character generator — 55+ automation modules and 700+ Python functions that assemble a complete, rules-legal character from 12 inputs (Flask · JavaScript · Docker), in production since 2023 and published for every FoundryVTT user. It implements two full third-party rule systems — Path of War and Spheres of Power/Might — on top of a deep campaign house-rule layer: feat-tax exemptions, mechanical flaws, trainers, an expanded profession sub-system and skill unlocks, each generated to be prerequisite-legal.

FoundryVTT PF1e tooling

A suite of small Foundry VTT modules and macros that smooth out Pathfinder 1e at the table — grouped one-click buffs, handy utility macros, multi-token buff distribution, and letting one character take multiple initiative slots. The newest scans a character and wires per-strike conditional modifiers — Path of War maneuvers, sphere talents and spells — onto every weapon they carry, with a per-weapon review pop-up, so nobody has to import a bulky palette actor or drag effects by hand.
